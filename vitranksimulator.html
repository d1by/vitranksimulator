Greetings!

At the outset, we thank for your interest in Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) and for participating in VITEEE-22 for admission to B.Tech. programmes. VITEEE-22 results will be available on 8th July 2022. The final rank is based on the equi-percentile ranking method. Please note that there is no rescheduling of VITEEE or reevaluation of the score sheet. Since ranking is based on percentile, the raw scores are not provided.

  • As you may be aware, admission to B.Tech programmes are purely on merit, based ONLY on VITEEE rank.
  • Applicants who have secured up to 1,00,000 ranks are eligible for attending the counselling in VIT Vellore, Chennai, VIT-AP and VIT Bhopal in four different phases as given under.
  • Applicants who have secured more than 1,00,000 ranks are eligible for attending the counselling in VIT-AP and VIT Bhopal only in the 5th Phase.
  • There is no separate management quota.
  • We do not charge any capitation fee.
  • We do not have any agents. Please do not believe any news/ message/ call offering such service.
  • Do not pay cash / DD to any fake agent.
  • If you avail any such unauthorized service, VIT shall not be responsible for the consequences.
  • All payments related to admission are to be made in favour of VIT, ONLY through online payment, in the admission portal. Cash will not be accepted.

Following is the brief outline of the admission process:

Step 1:      Know your rank by visiting the said portal.

Step 2:    Pay a non-refundable counselling fee of Rs.5900 (inclusive of GST) in the counselling portal before the due date specified for each phase of counselling. The payment facility will be available starting 8thJuly 22. Payment should be done on the day prior to the choice filling.

 

Step 3:    After payment of counselling fee, you can participate in the online counselling process, and fill in your choice of campus, programme and category.

  • Please note that once allotted, campus/ programme/ category cannot be changed.
  • Please ensure that you fill more than 25 choices (no limit maximum choices) to get an allotment.

Step 4:   Based on your rank and on the availability of seat, you will be allotted a programme, campus and category on the specified allotment date.

  • To confirm your allotment, you have the option to pay a part of the tuition fee as stated below or the full fee before the last date of each phase of counselling.
